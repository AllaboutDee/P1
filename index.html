Welcome to Surfing & Paradise


Are you passionate about Surfing? Or do you wish learn? Join us to meet like-minded people who love to surf, learn new tricks, travel and explore new destinations. Make new friends and create unforgettable memories! Instructors are on hand to share their skills. Families are welcome so you can learn and enjoy the waves together.

Our club hosts bi-weekly meetups at the best surfing spots in the UK. We also organize epic beach parties and exciting trips to surfing destinations, including our epic annual trip to Surfers Paradise, Australia.



Join the Waves

Why Join Surfing & Paradise?

Connect with Others

Meet fellow surfers who share your passion for the waves. Together, we build a close-knit community.

Stay Safe

Gain valuable tips on surfing techniques and safety, learn from seasoned surfers to make every session secure and fun.

Discover New Spots

Explore stunning beaches across the UK, and even world-famous surfing spots, with an enthusiastic crew.

Enjoy Surf Parties

Join us for gatherings, beach parties and bonfires after each surf session, creating great memories with friends.

Travel the World

Be part of our annual trips to breath-taking destinations such as Surfers Paradise, Australia—a dream surfing destination for any enthusiast!
